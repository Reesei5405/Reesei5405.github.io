Birthplace

Zoro was born in the East Blue, and grew up in Shimotsuki village.

He Learns his three sword style at a very young age and begins to refine the style as he gets older.

After Zoro left the village he makes a name for himself as "Pirate Hunter Zoro".

Later on Zoro gets "caught" by the navy but, gets saved by his future captain. He becomes the First crew mate and the right hand for his captain.
